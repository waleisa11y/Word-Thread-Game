Welcome to Word Thread

Here’s how to play:

  • Receive: You will be given a random word.
  • Weave: Write a new word using the last letter of the word you received to continue the thread.
  • Singular Focus: Only single words in the singular form are allowed.
  • No Repeats: Every word must be unique; you cannot use the same word twice in a single game.
  • Keep the Flow: Each successful word extends your chain and earns you points.
